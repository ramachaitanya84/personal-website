# Site Specification (MVP)

## Pages
1. Home
2. Profile
3. Resume
4. Projects
5. Writing
6. Now

## Home
- Primary interaction: prompt/search input
- Secondary: intent chips (Resume, Projects, Writing, Now)
- Goal: route users quickly to relevant content

## Profile
- Short bio
- Leadership narrative
- Key themes and areas of focus

## Projects
- List of selected projects
- Each project has its own detail page

## Writing
- Blog posts on product leadership, AI, judgment, systems

## Latest
- What I am currently building
- What roles or problems I am focused on

## Non-goals (for MVP)
- No authentication
- No comments
- No social feeds except LinkedIn
